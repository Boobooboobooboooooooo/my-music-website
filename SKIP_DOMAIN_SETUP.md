# How to Skip Domain Registration on Cloudflare

## ‚úÖ You DON'T Need a Domain!

Cloudflare Pages gives you a **FREE subdomain** automatically. Here's how to skip the domain step:

### Option 1: Look for "Skip" Button
- On the domain setup page, look for:
  - **"Skip"** button (usually at bottom)
  - **"Continue without domain"**
  - **"Use Cloudflare subdomain"**
  - **"Set up later"**

### Option 2: Leave Domain Field Empty
- If there's a text field for domain:
  - **Leave it completely empty**
  - Click **"Continue"** or **"Next"**

### Option 3: Use the Free Subdomain Option
- Look for:
  - **"Use Cloudflare Pages subdomain"**
  - **"Get a free .pages.dev domain"**
  - **"I'll set up a domain later"**

### What Happens Next:
After skipping, Cloudflare will:
1. Create your site automatically
2. Give you a free URL like: `https://my-music-website.pages.dev`
3. You can add a custom domain later (optional)
